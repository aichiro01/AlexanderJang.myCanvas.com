Many Faces:

With using the sculpting and manipulation abilities in Zbrush I produced a complete three-dimensional render of a few heads. These were done with no real vision, but fully motivated through creativity, and ideas on the spot. The first two represent humans and the third being a mixture of a goblin/demon.






Utopia:

In the program Blender I developed a three-dimensional background setting within a cave system, almost like an underground village. Each building embedded into the structure of the cave, along with a tunnel system that would lead to more villages below. It depicts the idea of a Utopia with its separation from the real world.






Ascent:

This piece was a fully three-dimensional animation based off a model I had made using Maya. The model, the scythe, and background were all done by hand. The model depicts a mixture of the grim reaper and a human. The animation shows the creature summoning his weapon as they then glide across the floor towards what the world holds in store for them.
