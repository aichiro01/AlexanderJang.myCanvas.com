Hardships:

This piece was done using my drawing skills in Adobe Illustrator. It displays all of which I have faced throughout the recent months of present year. Anger, resentment, anxiety, rage, frustration, and more. Hardships are a part of life and it's what makes us stronger moving forward, however it doesn't mean we struggle getting to the end.






How You Represent:

This piece is done in Adobe Illustrator displaying how people of a foreign countries/cultures should show interest in different cultures. This example depicts how people need to represent Asian culture with giving proper respect and credit. Social Justice have been the talk across several different social media platforms, and I felt this needed to be the next.
